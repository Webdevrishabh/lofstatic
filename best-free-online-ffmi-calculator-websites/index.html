25 Best Free Online FFMI Calculator Websites

Here is a list of the best free online FFMI calculator websites. FFMI is short for Fat-Free Mass Index. FFMI is a widely used metric to measure the lean mass and body fat ratio of an individual. This index calculates muscle mass relative to height. FFMI indicates muscle development. Thus it is used by bodybuilders as a means of measurement and comparison with others. FFMI is an alternative to BMI. Where BMI only accounts for the weight and height of the individual, FFMI takes into account lean muscle mass, making it a more reliable indicator of overall body composition. To calculate your FFMI, you should know your height, weight, and body fat percentage.

This post covers 25 free websites where you can calculate FFMI online. You get dedicated calculators on all these websites. Almost all these calculators work with imperial and metric unit systems. No matter what unit system you use, you can select the system of choice and go ahead with the calculations. Within seconds, you get the FFMI on the screen. Along with the FFMI, these calculators also get you the normalized or adjusted FFMI, lean mass or fat-free mass, and body fat. You can go through the post to explore these in detail.

All these calculators work almost the same way with the same input parameters and results. You can pick any of these and get the FFMI along with other related terms. Thus, picking a favorite is more of a choice rather that a decision based on features.
