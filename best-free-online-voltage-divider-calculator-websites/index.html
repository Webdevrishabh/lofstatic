22 Best Free Online Voltage Divider Calculator Websites

Here is a list of best free online voltage divider calculator websites. A Voltage Divider is a passive electronic circuit that reduces the output voltage based on the values of the resistors it carries. This circuit is used in various applications like Potentiometers, Reading Resistive Sensors, Level Shifting, and more. To calculate the exact output voltage a voltage divider circuit will produce, users need these online calculator websites.

These websites use the Input Voltage and Resistance values (R1 and R2) to find out the output voltage value. Besides this, some of these websites can also be used to find the Resistance and Input voltage of voltage divider circuits. Besides performing the calculation, they also offer voltage resistance calculation formulas along with voltage divider circuit diagrams. In some of these calculators, users can also manually change the SI units of Voltage and Resistance parameters.

For new users, I have included the steps of calculations in the description of each website. On these websites, users can also find additional online tools and calculators such as Ohm’s Law Calculator, MCD to Lumens Converter, BOM Tools, IC Design Tools, etc. Go through the list to know more about these websites.
