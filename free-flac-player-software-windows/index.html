26 Best Free FLAC Player Software For Windows

Here is a list of best free FLAC player software for Windows. FLAC or Free Lossless Audio Codec is an audio coding format used for lossless compression of digital audio files. Audio files compressed by FLAC algorithms consume 50 to 70 percent less space without compromising the audio quality. In order to play the FLAC audio files, you need special FLAC players. Hence, I have compiled a list of FLAC players that you can use to play FLAC audio files.

There are two types of FLAC players; some of them can play only audio files, while others can play both audio and video files. Not just FLAC audio but audio files of many other formats like WAV, MP3, MP2, AAC, OGG, etc. can also be played. As these players are basically music players which provide various handy tools. In many FLAC music players, you can easily create and export playlists in various formats like PLS, M3U, M3U8, etc. In some software, you can also import external playlists. Besides this, important audio tweaking features like Equalizer and Audio Filters are also present in most software. You can also easily adjust Pitch, Bass, Treble, etc. just by varying the bands of the Equalizer. Along with local audio files, you can also connect to and stream online radio using these software. Overall, all of these software provide a good experience of listening to FLAC audio.

How to play FLAC audio using FFMPEG:

In order to play a FLAC music file, you need to first write the path of ffplay.exe. After that, put a space and enter -i and then submit the path of music file under double quotes and press the Enter button to play. Your command will look like this:

ffplay.exe -i "E:\LISTOFFREEWARE.flac"

As soon as you submit the command, music will start playing in a separate audio player window. From the new window, you can also go forward and back by clicking on the left or right part of the audio player window.
