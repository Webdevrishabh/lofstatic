GPU Monitoring Software let you monitor Graphics card performance installed on your PC by providing accurate data along with real time statistics.

CAM

CAM is one of the best free GPU Monitoring software for Windows. This freeware provides you the information of Graphics card installed on your PC.

GPU-Z

GPU-Z  provides all the information related to your Graphics Card  including statistics of sensors along with graphs.

Geeks3D GPU Shark

Geeks3D GPU Shark provides GPU information in two modes: Simplified mode and Detailed mode. 

Open Hardware Monitor

Open Hardware Monitor not only provides the information of Graphics card, but also provides the information of CPU and memory usage of your system.

CPU-Z

CPU-Z lets you monitor the statistics of Graphics card along with specifications and statistics of CPU, Motherboard, and Memory.

GPU Temp

GPU Temp  is a simple GPU monitoring freeware in which you get important stats of GPU, like temperature and load.

CPUID Hardware Monitor

CPUID Hardware Monitor ilets you monitor the statistics of GPU and the information related to CPU and hard drive of your computer with real time values.

Piriform Speccy

Piriform Speccy displays the detailed information of CPU, Graphics card, Motherboard, Memory, and other peripherals connected to the computer.

GPU Tweak

GPU Tweak is a free software to keep an eye on the performance of your Graphics card including frequencies, voltages, etc.

Sidebar Diagnostics

Sidebar Diagnostics is a simple sidebar for Windows, which displays statistics of Graphics card, so that you can analyze the performance of Graphics card
