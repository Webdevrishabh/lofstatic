Open Source Video Converter Software For Windows

QWinFF

Use this open source software to convert video files of various formats like MP4, AVI, DIVX, MOV, and more.

WinFF

It is a cross-platform video converter that can convert video files of various formats and also adjust video parameters.

VLC Media Player

This open source video converter can convert videos of a variety of formats like MOV, MP4, FLV, AVI, MKV, etc.

HandBrake

This open-source software can encode as well as convert video files of various formats.

FFmpegYAG

Use it to batch convert multiple videos of one format to another video format.
