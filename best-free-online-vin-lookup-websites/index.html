16 Best Free Online VIN Lookup Websites

Here is a list of best free online VIN lookup websites. VIN Lookup or Vehicle Identification Number lookup is a process of finding out various information about a vehicle using its VIN number. Some of the information, a VIN lookup can find are vehicle specifications, number of times it has been sold, previous auction prices, accidental information, etc.

A VIN Number is a 17-digit alphanumeric code assigned to each vehicle that gives a vehicle a unique identity. To find out detailed information about a vehicle through its VIN number, users need these online VIN lookup websites.

These websites look up and show various types of information about a vehicle like Vehicle Name, Engine Specifications, Manufacturer Name, Vehicle Type, Vehicle Model name, Model Year, etc. Some of these websites can even show information related to previous accidents, auctions, vehicle auction prices, car theft, insurance, and safety. A few websites also teach users to manually decode a vehicle identification number. A complete vehicle report is also generated by some of these websites. Although, it is only available to premium users.

These websites also provide some additional information about a vehicle like future market value of a vehicle, price vs mileage graph, estimated vehicle value, and more. Go through the list to know more about these websites.

There are multiple locations on a vehicle where users can find look for VIN number namely Vehicle Dashboard, Driver's Side Door Jamb, Engine Compartment, and Vehicle Documents (title, registration papers, and insurance documents).

Yes, you can find vehicle specs using a VIN number. To do that, you can try one of the above-mentioned VIN lookup websites. Some of the vehicle specs that you can obtain using a VIN number are Vehicle Name, Engine Specifications, Manufacturer Name, Vehicle Type, Vehicle Model name, Model Year, etc.
