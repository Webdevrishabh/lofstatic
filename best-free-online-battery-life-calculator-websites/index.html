20 Best Free Online Battery Life Calculator Websites

Here is a list of the best free online battery life calculator websites. The life of a battery depends on the power consumption or load on the battery. A battery life calculator allows you to figure out how long a battery would last per charge cycle. This can be helpful for a mobile phone, laptop, EV, or any other electrical component.

This post covers 20 free websites where you can estimate battery life. All these websites feature online calculators to determine the runtime of a given battery based on consumption. To do that, you have to provide the battery capacity and the power consumption. Most of these calculators go with the assumption that the battery is continuously draining until it is discharged. Some calculators can also factor in sleep time when there is no consumption. This is useful to provide better estimation for devices like smartphones, laptops, etc. With that said, let’s go through the post to check these calculators in detail. I hope you can find a calculator that can help you accurately estimate the runtime of a battery for your particular scenario.

OmniCalculator.com

OmniCalculator.com offers a free online battery life calculator. This calculator helps you determine how long a battery would run. The calculator has a simple mode where it assumes that the battery runs continuously until it is discharged. For that, you have to provide the battery capacity and device consumption. You can also set a discharge safety percentage if you want. Then there is an advanced mode. It lets you analyze what happens if your device switches to sleep mode. In this mode, you can add the sleep and consumption during that time to get the battery runtime in hours. This calculator allows you to switch the measurement units for all applicable parameters. You can pick the unit of your choice and get the results as per your readings.

Highlights:

  • Output: Battery Runtime and Average Consumption.
  • Battery Type: N/A.
  • Additional Options: Inclusion of Sleep mode, Discharge Safety, and Awake time.
  • Change Unit: Yes, all parameters.
